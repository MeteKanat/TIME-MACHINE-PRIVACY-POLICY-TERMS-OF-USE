# Privacy Policy for Time Machine - Visual Time Capsule

**Last Updated: January 10, 2025**

## Introduction

Welcome to Time Machine ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide
- **Scene Descriptions**: Text you enter to describe historical scenes for image generation
- **Generated Content**: Images and videos created using our AI service
- **App Preferences**: Language, appearance settings, and notification preferences

### 2. Automatically Collected Information
- **Location Data**: With your permission, we access your device location to help you explore historical places on the map. This data is processed locally and not stored on our servers.
- **Device Information**: Basic device information for app functionality and crash reporting

### 3. Usage Data
- **Credits Usage**: We track credit consumption for subscription management
- **Feature Usage**: Anonymous analytics to improve app experience

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Generate AI-powered historical images and videos
- Provide location-based historical exploration features
- Manage your subscription and credits
- Improve and optimize our services
- Send notifications (with your consent)

## Third-Party Services

### AI Image Generation (Fal AI)
We use Fal AI services to generate images and videos. When you create content:
- Your text prompts are sent to Fal AI servers
- Generated images/videos are returned to your device
- Fal AI's privacy policy applies to their processing

**Fal AI Privacy Policy**: https://fal.ai/privacy

### Apple Services
- **StoreKit**: For in-app purchases and subscriptions
- **MapKit**: For map display (no data sent to Apple beyond standard MapKit usage)
- **Core Location**: Location data stays on your device

## Data Storage

### Local Storage
- Generated images and videos are stored locally on your device
- App settings and preferences are stored locally
- Memory/favorites data is stored locally using UserDefaults

### Cloud Storage
- We do NOT store your personal data on external servers
- We do NOT have user accounts or cloud sync
- All your content remains on your device

## Data Sharing

We do NOT:
- Sell your personal data
- Share your data with third parties for marketing
- Create user profiles for advertising

We may share data only:
- With Fal AI for content generation (text prompts only)
- If required by law

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View all data stored in the app
- **Delete**: Clear all history and data from Settings → Clear History
- **Control**: Manage location permissions in device settings
- **Opt-out**: Disable notifications at any time

## Data Retention

- **Local Data**: Retained until you delete it or uninstall the app
- **AI Prompts**: Not retained by us after image generation
- **Subscription Data**: Managed by Apple through App Store

## Children's Privacy

Time Machine is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such data, please contact us.

## Security

We implement appropriate security measures:
- All API communications use HTTPS encryption
- Local data is protected by iOS security features
- No sensitive data is transmitted to our servers

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- App update notifications for significant changes
